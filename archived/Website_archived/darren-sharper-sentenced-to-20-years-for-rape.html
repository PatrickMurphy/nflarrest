Darren Sharper Sentenced for Rape

Darren Sharper played 14 seasons of NFL football. After being drafted by the Green Bay Packers in 1997, he also played for the Minnesota Vikings and the New Orleans Saints. After being arrested in January 17, 2014 in California he has been sentanced to an additional 20 years in prison.

The Associated Press reported in August that Sharper drugged and raped potentially up to 16 women in Nevada, California, Louisiana and Arizona.
